Topics

What you will learn.

  • Day 1

    The power of AI

    We separate fact from fiction with a practical introduction to Artificial Intelligence. You will see first-hand how AI technologies are transforming the business landscape and the implications this has for your own business. By the end of this session you will have an understanding of why it is critical for you to develop your own strategic plan for the adoption and use of AI technologies.

  • Day 2

    Leveraging AI

    You are given an overview of key AI technologies and shown how you can use them to transform your business. As part of an ongoing project, you will start to build your own Artificial Intelligence Strategic Plan and continue to build on it throught each webinar

  • Day 3

    Machine Learning

    we begin to explore each key AI technology in detail, starting with Machine Learning. See how deep learning is being applied to a wide range of applications, from business intelligence and predictive analytics to self-driving cars to medical and legal applications. We then explore ideas for the application of Machine Learning for your strategic plan.

  • Day 4

    Natural Language Processing

    we examine conversational user interfaces, speech recognition and synthesis and their application to business processes and automation. We take a special look at the rise of chatbots and how chatbot technology is revolutionising business and customer experience. We conclude by considering the application of these technologies to your business.

  • Day 5

    Internet Of Things

    We look at the incredible explosion of the Internet of Things, what it is and how it is being used. We take a close look at the intersection between Artificial Intelligence and the data produced by IOT devices and the business opportunities it offers. As usual we conclude by considering ways in which IOT can improve your business.

  • Day 6

    AI Powerd Robotics

    AI combined with automation is changing the way companies design, build and deliver their products and services, causing CEOs to re-think how automation can be used as a competitive advantage.

  • Day 7

    Putting your strategic plan into action

    In this session we finalise your strategic AI plan. You will be given the tools you need to start putting your strategic plan into action. You will be shown AI technologies that you can start using TODAY, how to find and leverage third party AI service providers relevant to your business and considerations around outsourcing talent or growing your AI skills within your organisation.
